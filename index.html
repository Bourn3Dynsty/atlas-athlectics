    About Us

    At Atlas Athletics, we strive to share the benefits of harnessing human health, and our goal is to promote a culture of wellness. Like the Greeks, I believe that a strong and healthy body is the foundation for a happy and fulfilling life; That's why I offer a range of products and services designed to help individuals of all ages and fitness levels reach their goals.

    Who am I?

    My name is Cole Coburn, I am a Certified Personal Trainer and Nutritionist from Farmersville, TX

    I have had a passion for health for about ten years, starting with my time in the Marine Corps. It was there that I learned the advantages of optimizing human health and just how much more enjoyable life can be when you’re built to handle anything it throws at you. Whether it's carrying the load for your community or for your family; join Atlas Athletics and harness your potential!
